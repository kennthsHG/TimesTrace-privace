# TimesTrace Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 25, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TimesTrace (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

**Core Value**: An offline-first habit tracking and focus timer app that helps you record habits, focus sessions, and diary entries, with statistics and daily review features. No account registration required.

---

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Information We Collect

The App may collect the following types of information:

| Information Type | Purpose | Required |
|-----------------|---------|----------|
| Habit Data | Name, icon, color, daily target, category, reminders, chaining | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Focus Session Records | Start/end time, duration, mood, diary text, notes | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Diary Content | Simple diary text and rich diary (images, mood, weather tags) | Yes (Core Feature) |
| Habit Goals | Title, type, target value, start/end dates, status | Yes (Core Feature) |
| App Preferences | Theme, language, sound, screen always-on, daily review time | No |
| Subscription Status | Pro subscription type and expiry (managed by platform store) | No |
| Notification Settings | Timer completion alert and daily review reminder time | No |

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Your name, email address, or other personal identification information
- Your precise location information
- Your contacts or social relationship data
- Your financial or payment card information
- Device identifiers or advertising identifiers

---

## 3. Permissions

### 3.1 Notification Permission

**Purpose**: Allows the app to send timer completion alerts and daily review reminders.

**Required**: No, denying this permission does not affect other features.

### 3.2 Photo Library Permission

**Purpose**: Allows the app to add images in rich diary entries.

**Required**: No, denying this permission allows normal use of other features.

### 3.3 Screen Always-On

**Purpose**: Keeps the screen awake during focus timer sessions.

**Required**: No, can be disabled in app settings.

---

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Local Storage

- All data (habits, focus sessions, diary entries, goals, settings) is stored in a local database (Hive) on your device
- Diary images are stored in the app's document directory on your device
- We do not upload any of your data to our own servers
- Uninstalling the app will delete all local data and it cannot be recovered

### 4.2 Offline-First Design

The App is designed to work offline:

- Habit tracking: Works completely offline
- Focus timer: Works completely offline
- Diary entries: Works completely offline (images stored locally)
- Statistics & daily review: Works completely offline
- Habit goals: Works completely offline

### 4.3 Cloud Sync

Data models include cloud sync fields, but no cloud sync feature is enabled in the current version. If enabled in the future, we will update this policy and seek your consent.

### 5.2 No Advertising or Analytics SDK

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information:

- All data is stored within the app sandbox, protected by the operating system's security mechanisms
- Since we do not transmit your data to any server, there is no risk of data leakage during network transmission
- We recommend using system backup features (iCloud Backup / Google Backup) to protect your data

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has used this app without authorization, please contact us.

## 8. Data Retention
